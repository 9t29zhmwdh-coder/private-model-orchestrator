## Why dual-licensing

The Community Edition stays genuinely open source and is not restricted by
this model. The Enterprise Edition targets organizations that need
fleet-wide management, multi-tenant support, or compliance/reporting
capabilities beyond what a single-instance, self-hosted tool provides, and
funds continued development of both editions.
